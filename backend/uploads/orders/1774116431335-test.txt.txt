IN THE COURT OF THE DISTRICT AND SESSIONS JUDGE, ERNAKULAM

Case No: 145/2024
Petitioner: Mr. Arjun Menon
Respondent: Mr. Rakesh Nair

ORDER

1. The present petition has been filed by the petitioner seeking relief in respect of a property dispute concerning residential premises situated at Marine Drive, Ernakulam.

2. The petitioner submits that the respondent has unlawfully occupied a portion of the property without consent and has failed to vacate despite repeated notices dated 12.01.2024 and 25.02.2024.

3. The respondent contends that possession was granted under a verbal lease agreement and denies any illegal occupation. However, no written agreement has been produced before this Court.

4. The Court has perused the documents placed on record including the property title deed, tax receipts, electricity bills, and legal notice copies.

5. Upon preliminary examination, the Court observes that the title deed stands solely in the name of the petitioner. The respondent has not furnished documentary evidence to establish tenancy rights.

6. Considering the submissions made by both parties, the matter requires detailed evidence examination including witness statements and cross-examination.

7. The Court hereby directs both parties to maintain status quo with respect to possession of the disputed premises until final adjudication.

8. The respondent is directed to file a written statement along with supporting documents within 30 days from the date of this order.

9. The petitioner may file rejoinder, if any, within 15 days thereafter.

10. The next hearing is scheduled on 15th November 2024 for framing of issues.

11. Parties are directed to appear in person or through authorized counsel on the next date of hearing.

12. Any violation of the status quo order shall invite appropriate legal consequences as per law.

Ordered accordingly.

Date: 10th October 2024
